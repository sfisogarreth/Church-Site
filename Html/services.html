Men's Fellowship

Time: Every Saturday, 10 AM - 12 PM

The Men's Fellowship is a space for men to come together, share their experiences, and grow in faith. Join us for discussions, prayers, and support as we navigate the challenges of life with the guidance of God's Word.

Women's Fellowship

Time: Every Wednesday, 5 PM - 7 PM

The Women's Fellowship is dedicated to empowering women in their spiritual journey. We provide a supportive environment for women to connect, share, and grow through Bible studies, worship, and fellowship activities.
