About Our Church

Located at the corner of Doon Avenue and Freeway Bible Kerk, AFM House of Prayer Boksburg has been a beacon of hope and faith for seven years...

Under the visionary leadership of Pastor FN Mazivanhanga, we have grown in both numbers and spiritual depth.

Message from Our Pastor

“For seven years, AFM House of Prayer has been a place where lives are transformed.”

At AFM House of Prayer, we stand firm in our faith in Jesus Christ and His teachings. Our mission is to build a strong and supportive community of believers who show love and compassion to everyone. We believe in the transformative power of faith and the importance of nurturing a deep, personal relationship with God.

Our traditions are rooted in the teachings of the Bible, and we uphold the practice of baptism by immersion, symbolizing the believer's death to sin and resurrection to new life in Christ. We also practice dedication for infants and young children, committing to raise them in the ways of the Lord while the congregation prays for their spiritual growth and protection.

We are committed to empowering the next generation to walk in faith and purpose, and we strive to serve with love, faith, and commitment to God's people. Our church is a beacon of hope, faith, and transformation, and we look forward to many more years of building lives, nurturing faith, and spreading God's love.

Message from Our Deputy Pastor

“Leadership is about serving with love, faith, and commitment to God's people.”

At AFM House of Prayer, we believe that true leadership is rooted in service. As your Deputy Pastor, my mission is to serve our congregation with unwavering love, faith, and dedication. Our church is built on the foundation of strong, compassionate leadership that seeks to uplift and support every member of our community.

We uphold the teachings of Jesus Christ, emphasizing the importance of humility, kindness, and selfless service. Our traditions are deeply rooted in the Bible, and we strive to live out these values in our daily lives. Whether through acts of charity, community outreach, or spiritual guidance, we are committed to making a positive impact on the lives of those around us.

As we continue to grow and evolve as a church, I encourage each of you to embrace the spirit of service and leadership. Together, we can create a community that reflects the love and grace of God, and inspire others to join us on this journey of faith.

Message from Our Youth Pastor

“Empowering the next generation to walk in faith and purpose.”

At AFM House of Prayer, our youth ministry is dedicated to nurturing the spiritual growth and development of our young people. We believe that the youth are not just the future of the church but also its vibrant present. Our mission is to empower the next generation to walk confidently in their faith and to discover their God-given purpose.

Our teachings are rooted in the Word of God, emphasizing the importance of personal relationship with Jesus Christ, moral integrity, and community service. We encourage our youth to be active participants in their faith journey, to seek wisdom and guidance through prayer, and to cultivate a lifestyle that reflects Christ's love and compassion.

We offer various programs and activities designed to engage and inspire our youth, including Bible study sessions, worship nights, outreach projects, and fellowship events. Through these initiatives, we aim to create a supportive and nurturing environment where young people can grow spiritually, develop leadership skills, and build lasting friendships.

Join us as we journey together, empowering the next generation to make a lasting impact in their communities and beyond. Together, we can build a legacy of faith, hope, and love.
