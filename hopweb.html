Baptism and Dedication in the AFM Church

  In the Apostolic Faith Mission (AFM) Church, baptism and dedication are seen as significant milestones in a believer's 
  journey of faith. The AFM Church upholds the biblical practice of baptism by immersion, symbolizing the believer's 
  death to sin and resurrection to new life in Christ (Romans 6:4). It is an outward declaration of an inward 
  transformation and commitment to follow Jesus Christ. For infants and young children, the AFM Church practices 
  dedication rather than baptism.Dedication is a solemn act where parents present their child to God, committing to 
  raise them in the ways of the Lord while the congregation prays for the child’s spiritual growth and protection. 
  The AFM Church believes that baptism is a personal decision made by individuals who have reached an age of 
  accountability and have consciously chosen to follow Christ. These practices reflect the AFM Church's commitment
  to nurturing a deep and personal relationship with God, rooted in faith, obedience, and community support.
  
  At AFM House of Prayer, we believe in the importance of supporting and uplifting our community. One of the
  ways we do this is through our Church Basket Collection. This initiative is designed to gather resources and donations 
  from our congregation to help those in need within our church and the wider community.Every Sunday, during our worship 
  services, we set aside time for the Church Basket Collection. This is an opportunity for our members to contribute 
  non-perishable food items, clothing, and other essential supplies. These donations are then distributed to individuals 
  and families who are facing difficult times, ensuring that they have the necessary support and resources. This act of 
  giving is rooted in our commitment to live out the teachings of Jesus Christ. We are called to love and serve one 
  another, and through the Church Basket Collection, we are able to demonstrate our care and compassion for our fellow 
  believers. It is a tangible way to show God's love and make a positive impact on the lives of others. We encourage all 
  our members to participate in this meaningful initiative. Your generous contributions, no matter how small, make a 
  significant difference in the lives of those in need. Together, we can build a stronger, more supportive community 
  that reflects the love and grace of God.
